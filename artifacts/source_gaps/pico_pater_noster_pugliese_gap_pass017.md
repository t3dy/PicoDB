# Source Gap: Pico's Pater Noster Commentary and Benivieni Translation

- Artifact ID: `gap_pico_pater_noster_pugliese_pass017`
- Type: source gap
- Status: ACQUISITION_PRIORITY
- Evidence status: high

## Work

Pico's *Expositio singularis in Orationem Dominicam* / commentary on the *Pater noster* should be added to PicoDB's primary-text acquisition queue.

## Why It Matters

Pugliese shows that Benivieni produced a vernacular Florentine translation of the work and that this translation may preserve evidence relevant to the Latin text's transmission. The Laurenziana witness appears especially important because it has Benivieni-related marginalia and may preserve a more authoritative or earlier state than later printed or copied witnesses.

## Known Witnesses and Leads

- Laurenziana, Fondo Acquisti e Doni 55: Benivieni manuscript context with the vernacular translation.
- Tours, Bibliotheque municipale, MS 2110: related witness from the Gondi material.
- Toronto, Fisher Rare Books Library, Buchanan Collection: related witness from the Gondi material.
- Basel 1557 Opera: printed Latin control witness already in PicoDB as OCR.
- Bologna posthumous print and later early modern Italian translations: require control.

## Open Problems

- Whether Pico wrote more than one Latin version of the *Expositio*.
- Whether Benivieni translated from an earlier or otherwise divergent Latin state.
- Whether the printed Latin text contains errors or late additions.
- Whether the Benivieni translation can help reconstruct a better text.
